MUTHOOT INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND SCIENCE


Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is promoted by Muthoot M. George Institute of Technology, a Section 25 Company within the Muthoot Group. MITS is a self financing technical institution offering postgraduate and undergraduate engineering programmes.

College website: http://mgmits.ac.in/

VISION

To be a centre of excellence for learning and research in engineering and technology, producing intellectually well-equipped and socially committed citizens possessing an ethical value system.
